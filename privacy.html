Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Who We Are

Wrong Way Studios operates the mobile game "Wrong Way: Don't be mad" (the "App"). Contact: wrongwayapp.official@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the game:

  • Account data: Email address and/or Google account ID (via Firebase Authentication), chosen display name.
  • Game data: ELO rating, XP, level, coins, owned items (skins, banners), achievements, match history, friend list.
  • Player tag: A short randomly generated player ID (e.g. #ABC234), not linked to your real name.
  • Technical data: IP address (retained temporarily for security and anti-abuse purposes only, not stored long-term), device type, app version.
  • Error reports: If a technical error occurs, the app automatically stores a short report (error message, affected code location, browser/device type, app version, time) under your user ID — at most 10 entries per account, used only to fix bugs. You can also voluntarily send a problem report after an online match (your description, the match history and technical match data) — at most 3 per day. Deleted together with your account.
  • Chat messages: In-game text chat. Not stored permanently after the match ends. When something is reported, the reported message and the match's chat history are kept for review as context (see section 5).

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, or any sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the game and synchronize your progress across devices.
  • To display leaderboards and match results.
  • To prevent cheating, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • To send important service notifications (e.g. account security).

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising profiling.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (Google LLC) — Authentication, Realtime Database. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Railway — Game server hosting. Railway Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.) — web hosting for the browser version and this page.
  • Google AdMob (planned) — optional rewarded ads. A consent banner (Google UMP) will ask for permission before any ads are shown; no personalized ads without consent.

International transfers (GDPR Ch. V / KVKK Art. 9): data is processed on servers in the EU and the USA (Google, GitHub, Railway). US transfers are based on EU Standard Contractual Clauses. By using the App you expressly consent to this transfer as required by Turkish KVKK Art. 9.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account exists. You can request deletion at any time (see section 7). Temporary data (IP addresses, match logs) is deleted automatically within 30 days. Error reports are deleted together with your account. Reports about rule violations and ban records are kept after account deletion to prevent ban evasion (legitimate interest, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR). Chat excerpts in reports and moderation logs are removed after 12 months at the latest.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights — Account Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time.

To delete your account and all associated data: Open the App → Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently deletes all your data from our servers (except abuse-prevention records — see section 5).

You may also send a deletion request to: wrongwayapp.official@gmail.com. For full details on what is deleted and what is retained, see our account deletion page.

Under GDPR (EU users) and KVKK (Turkish users), you also have the right to data portability, restriction of processing, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over TLS encryption. Game-critical data (coins, items, achievements) is stored exclusively on our server using Firebase Admin SDK — clients cannot modify these values directly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent version. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact: Wrong Way Studios — wrongwayapp.official@gmail.com
